Personal Info

I'm a software developer originally from St. Louis, Missouri and currently living in Atlanta, Georgia.

I came to tech after 6+ years as a medical laboratory scientist. I was inspired to switch careers after experiencing the collision of medical crises with planned downtime; we were incredibly hamstrung in medicine whenever tech limitations impeded our efforts to save lives.

After completing DigitalCrafts' 16-week full-time development bootcamp, I joined the amazing Lumen Learning and have been with them since July 2021. I'm incredibly grateful for the opportunity to work on products aimed at improving outcomes for low-income and underrepresented college students across the country. I have learned so much in my time with them already, and can't wait to see what's next!
